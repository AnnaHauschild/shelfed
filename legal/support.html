Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use Shelfed?
No. Shelfed works without any login or account. Everything you add — watched titles, favourites, watchlist and your shelf — is stored locally on your device.

How do I add a title to my shelf?
Swipe through the Discover deck, or use Search to find any film, series or book. Mark it as watched or add it to your shelf, favourites or watchlist.

Where does the movie, series and book information come from?
Film and TV data is provided by The Movie Database (TMDB), and book data by Open Library. Shelfed only reads this public information — it does not send any personal data to these services.

Will I lose my shelf if I get a new phone?
Because your data is stored on the device, uninstalling the app or switching devices will start you with a fresh shelf. Please keep this in mind before removing the app.

How do I delete my data?
Uninstalling Shelfed removes all stored data. You can also clear the app's storage in your device settings to reset the shelf without removing the app.
